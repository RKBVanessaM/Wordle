Here are the rules:

You have 6 attempts to guess a secret 5-letter English word.
Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was.
A green tile means the letter is in the correct spot.
A yellow tile means the letter is in the word but in a different spot.
A grey tile means the letter is not in the word at all.
Letters may appear more than once in the word.
The goal is to guess the correct word within 6 tries.
